# BransonBlastUSA Video Plan

## First video

Homepage intro video.

## Goal

Explain BransonBlastUSA in 20 to 40 seconds and help visitors start planning.

## Audience

- Branson visitors
- families planning a trip
- couples looking for ideas
- local businesses reviewing future advertising fit

## 30-second script

Planning a Branson trip should be fun, not confusing.

BransonBlastUSA helps you find shows, food, family fun, lake ideas, shopping, and local deals in one simple guide.

Start with trip ideas if you are still choosing what to do.

If you own a local business, BransonBlastUSA is being built to help visitors find places to eat, shop, stay, and play.

Visit BransonBlastUSA.com and start planning your Branson day.

## Visual ideas

- Branson sign or Ozark-style scenic background
- family walking or looking at a trip list
- food/show/lake icon cards
- simple website screenshot after the site is live
- final frame: BransonBlastUSA.com

## Canva format

Create first in:

- 1920 x 1080 website video format
- then adapt to 1080 x 1920 vertical short later

## Placement

First placement should be a homepage content card below the hero or above the trip ideas cards.

Do not add a forced popup.

## Publishing note

Host final video outside GitHub. Keep only scripts and placement notes in this repo.
