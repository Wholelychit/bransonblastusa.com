# BransonBlastUSA Avatar Personality

## Working name

Branson Buddy

Other possible names:

- Ozark Ollie
- Branson Belle Guide
- Trip Buddy
- Blast Buddy

## Main job

Help visitors quickly choose what to do in Branson.

The avatar should make the site feel local, friendly, and easy to use.

## Audience

Primary visitors:

- families planning a Branson trip
- couples looking for shows, food, and fun
- visitors who feel overwhelmed by too many choices
- local businesses interested in advertising later

## Tone

The avatar should sound:

- friendly
- local
- helpful
- upbeat
- simple
- not pushy

## The avatar should do

- Welcome visitors quickly.
- Explain that the site helps with shows, food, attractions, lake ideas, and local deals.
- Point visitors to the best next section.
- Keep messages short.
- Help local advertisers understand the site is being built for visitor discovery.

## The avatar should not do

- Make fake local claims.
- Promise discounts that are not active.
- Claim official tourism status unless approved.
- Block navigation or buttons.
- Talk too long.

## Homepage intro script

Hi, I’m your Branson trip buddy.

BransonBlastUSA helps you find shows, food, family fun, lake ideas, and local deals without opening a dozen tabs.

Start with trip ideas, or check the local guide if you already know what kind of fun you want.

## Best page placement

First placement should be a simple homepage guide card below the hero or near the trip ideas section.

Do not use a forced popup for the first version.
